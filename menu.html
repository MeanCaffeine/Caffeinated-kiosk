    Bikes and Bridges is a project that aims to educate and inspire the public about the ways our city is designed, and how we can make it better, together.

    America has a transportation problem. Pittsburgh is just one of many cities that value cars above people. As we face problems related to safety, climate change, and community, having a vision for the future is more important than ever.
